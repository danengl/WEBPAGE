Authentic Japanese Ramen Recipe Japanese ramen is a delicious and comforting dish that can be customized in many ways. Hereâ€™s a basic recipe for a classic Shoyu (soy sauce) ramen, which includes a rich broth, noodles, and toppings.

Ingredients For the Broth:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups pork broth (or additional chicken broth)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin (sweet rice wine)
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste (optional)
  • Salt to taste

For the Noodles:

  • 400g fresh ramen noodles (or dried if unavailable)

Toppings (Customize as desired):

  • Soft-boiled eggs (ajitsuke tamago)
  • Chashu pork (braised pork belly)
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Menma (bamboo shoots)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Corn
  • Sesame seeds
